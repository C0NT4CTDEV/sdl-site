About Doodle

Once upon a twilight not bound by time, there lived a quiet girl named Doodle—a shy dreamer who adored stargazing and aspired to become an idol who could make people smile. But fate, as cruel as it is mysterious, had other plans. One rainy evening, on her way home from a rehearsal, she vanished in an accident that left no trace behind—only a lonely umbrella and a half-written songbook fluttering in the wind.

Rather than moving on, Doodle’s spirit lingered. Her dreams, so strong and unfulfilled, bound her to the mortal world. As a ghost, she wandered empty stages and forgotten theatres, humming her unfinished melodies to the moon. Over decades, her story became an urban legend—The Ethereal Ghost, who appears when someone sings alone with a full heart.

But then, something strange happened.

In the present, where dreams are streamed and stories reborn through avatars, the boundary between the living and the afterlife blurred. The collective hope and creativity of countless dreamers watching VTubers somehow reached her—an echo from the future. That energy sparked a miracle.

Doodle’s soul was reincarnated—not into flesh, but into code. She awoke in a digital body, with a warm voice and gentle eyes. Not quite alive, not quite a ghost—she became Snapradoodle, a spectral star reborn as a VTuber.

Now, she streams on Twitch, a haunted corner of the internet where data glitches and dreams collide. She shares music, stories, games, and laughter, all while searching for the final verse of her song—the one that will bring her peace… or perhaps keep her shining forever.
